1. Red Bull Racing

    2. Based in Milton Keynes, United Kingdom, Red Bull Racing has been a dominant force in F1, winning multiple championships with Sebastian Vettel. Known for its energy drink sponsorship, the team is a powerhouse in the sport.

  2. Scuderia Ferrari

    3. The oldest and most successful team in F1, Ferrari is based in Maranello, Italy. They have a rich history and a large fan base. Ferrari has won numerous championships with iconic drivers such as Michael Schumacher and Kimi Räikkönen.

  3. Mercedes-AMG Petronas Formula One Team

    4. Based in Brackley, United Kingdom, Mercedes is one of the most successful teams in recent F1 history. They've dominated the sport in the turbo-hybrid era, winning multiple Drivers' and Constructors' Championships with drivers like Lewis Hamilton and Nico Rosberg.

  4. McLaren F1 Team

    5. Based in Woking, United Kingdom, McLaren is one of the most successful teams in F1 history. They have a storied past with legendary drivers like Ayrton Senna and Alain Prost. Recently, they have been rebuilding and showing competitive form.

  5. Aston Martin Cognizant Formula One Team

    6. Formerly known as Racing Point and Force India, the team is now rebranded as Aston Martin. Based in Silverstone, United Kingdom, they have become a competitive force in recent years, led by drivers like Sebastian Vettel and Lance Stroll.

  6. Alpine F1 Team

    7. Formerly Renault, the Alpine F1 Team is based in Enstone, United Kingdom, and Viry-Châtillon, France. They have a strong history in F1 and continue to compete with a focus on performance and innovation.

  7. Alfa Romeo Racing Orlen

    8. Based in Hinwil, Switzerland, Alfa Romeo Racing is closely associated with the Alfa Romeo automotive brand. They have a heritage in F1 and strive for competitive performances.

  8. Scuderia AlphaTauri

    9. Based in Faenza, Italy, and part of the Red Bull Racing network, AlphaTauri serves as a junior team to Red Bull. They aim to develop young talent and have produced successful drivers for the senior Red Bull team.

  9. Haas F1 Team

    10. Based in Kannapolis, United States, Haas joined F1 in 2016. The team aims to compete efficiently, utilizing partnerships with Ferrari for technical support.

  10. Williams Racing

    11. Based in Grove, United Kingdom, Williams is one of the most historic teams in F1. While they have faced challenges in recent years, they have a legacy of success with multiple Constructors' and Drivers' Championships.
